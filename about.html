About Us

Bringing Education to the Doorsteps

🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality foundational education to every child living in urban slum communities, completely free of cost.

👁️

Our Vision

A society where a child's socio-economic background does not dictate the quality of education they receive.

We operate by setting up small learning spaces directly within underserved neighborhoods. We recruit passionate local teachers, train them extensively, and provide all necessary learning materials.

Our Story

The Journey of Gyan Shala

Since our inception, we have relentlessly pursued the goal of bringing quality education to those who need it most. We believe every child deserves a chance to shine.

Our Timeline

  • 2010: Founded with a single learning center and 20 students.
  • 2015: Expanded to 50 centers across multiple districts.
  • 2020: Launched digital learning initiatives during the pandemic.
  • 2026: Empowering over 25,000 students through 1,200+ active centers.
